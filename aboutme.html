About Me

I am currently interested in a career change from working on vehicles since I was a teenager to something that interests me alot and that I can apply my problem-solving skills in. Programming is very unique and I enjoy learning something new from it everyday. My goal is to take all the knowledge I have gained from college and the full stack bootcamp and take those tools to apply my years of problem-solving and translate it to all work in the Full Stack industry.

My hobbies include playing video games on my computer, continuing learning how to be a future full stack developer, and spending time with my family. I enjoy watching professional played video games such as: CSGO, LOL, Smite, and Rainbow Siege. I also enjoy traditional sports, my favorite teams are: Chicago Cubs and White Sox in the MLB, Notre Dame Fighting Irish in college football, and Chicago Blackhawks in hockey.
